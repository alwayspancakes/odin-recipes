Tiramisu

Description

This is a recipe for Tiramisu which I found on the internet.

All credit goes to the original author of this recipe, Tasts Better From Scratch

I chose this recipe because I have been craving for Tiramisu but unable to fulfill the craving because I do not live close to any Tiramisu source.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfingers
  • Mascarpone
  • Heavy whipped cream
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Mix creamy filling Beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar and vanilla together until stiff peaks.
  2. Dip ladyfingers and add the espressoto a shallow bowl and dip the ladyfingers on both sides.
