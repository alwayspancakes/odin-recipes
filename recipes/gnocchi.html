Parmesan Gnocchi

Description

This is a recipe for Gnocchi which I found on the internet.

This is the third recipe I found on the internet for this project

Ingredients

  • 300g gnochhi
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 60g parmesan
  • 2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the gnocchi and drain the water, and reserve 200ml of the cooking water
  2. Heat the butter in the frying pan and add the gnocchi, cheese and pepper, and 150ml of the cooking water
  3. Stir the gnocchi until it is coated. Pour in more of the reserved cooking water if you prefer more sauce
  4. Season to taste
